How to Play

You are dealt 12 letter tiles — 3 vowels and 9 consonants — determined by a seed word. Drag tiles from the tile bank onto the grid to form words.

  • Words can go horizontally or vertically
  • Words must be at least 3 letters by default (2-letter words can be enabled in CONFIG)
  • You do not need to place all tiles
  • Words use the official Scrabble dictionary (TWL06)

Scoring

Each letter has a Scrabble point value (A=1, P=3, Z=10, etc.). Only valid dictionary words count. Invalid words appear crossed out. By default, only the highest-scoring connected group of words counts — see Game Settings below.

Play Tab Buttons

  • RESET — Clears the board and restarts the timer
  • RECENTER — Moves all placed tiles to the center of the grid
  • SCRAMBLE — Randomly shuffles tiles remaining in the bank
  • SAVE — Saves the current board and timer to your history
  • CHECK WORDS — Validates all words on the board and shows your current score without ending the game
  • FINISH — Ends the game, locks the board, and shows your final score. A Share button is availble.

Sharing Results

After tapping CHECK WORDS or FINISH, tap 🔗 SHARE in the results dialog. On mobile this opens your share sheet (messages, email, etc.). On desktop it copies to your clipboard. The share text includes your seed, time, each word with (✅/❌), and total score. If words are in separate groups, a ⚠️ note is added.

CONFIG Tab

  • Daily Challenge — Everyone who plays on the same calendar day gets the same 12 tiles
  • Random Seed — Picks a random word from a built-in list of 50 seeds, giving you a new set of tiles each time
  • Custom Seed — Type any word (3+ letters) for a specific tile set. Share the seed with friends so you can all play the same game and compare scores!
  • Recent Games — Reload and continue a previously saved game

Game Settings

  • Allow 2-letter words — When checked, 2-letter Scrabble words (AA, BI, XI, etc.) are valid and score normally. Off by default.
  • Require all words connected — When checked, all your words must physically touch (like a crossword) for all of them to score. When unchecked (default), only the highest-scoring connected group of words counts toward your total — isolated words are shown grayed out with ⚠️.
